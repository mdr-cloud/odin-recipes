Plain Rice

Description:

Rice, edible starchy cereal grain and the plant by which it is produced.

Ingredients:

  • uncooked rice

Instructions:

  1. Rinse rice
  2. Put rice in a pot one (1) part rice and two (2) parts water.
  3. Bring your rice to a boil on high heat.
  4. Once the water is boiling, cover the rice and bring down the heat to low.
  5. Let cook for fifteen (15) minutes.
  6. Let rest and serve.
