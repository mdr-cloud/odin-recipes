Hard Boiled Egg

Description:

A hard boiled egg is boiled so that both the yolk and white are fully cooked and solid.

Ingredients:

  • One (1) egg

Instructions:

  1. Take egg out of fridge.
  2. Place egg in boiling water.
  3. Boil egg for twelve minutes.
  4. Remove egg from boiling water.
  5. Let egg cool for minutes before eating.
